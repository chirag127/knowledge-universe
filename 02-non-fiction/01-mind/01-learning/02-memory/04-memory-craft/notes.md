### Author Background
- **Dr. Lynne Kelly**: Science educator, researcher, and writer based in Australia
- PhD in memory research; adjunct research fellow at La Trobe University
- Author of *The Memory Code* (2016) and *Memory Craft* (2019)
- Known for her pioneering research connecting indigenous knowledge systems with mnemonic techniques
- Has personally tested all memory methods she writes about
