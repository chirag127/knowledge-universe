### Authors Background
- **Harry Lorayne**: World-renowned memory training specialist, magician, and author of 10+ books on memory. Performed memory feats on TV and for corporate clients worldwide. Called "the Yoda of memory training" by some reviewers.
- **Jerry Lucas**: NBA Hall of Fame forward (Cincinnati Royals, New York Knicks, Golden State Warriors). Olympic gold medalist (1960). Known as "Luke" and one of the smartest players in NBA history. Applied memory techniques to basketball playbooks and statistics.
