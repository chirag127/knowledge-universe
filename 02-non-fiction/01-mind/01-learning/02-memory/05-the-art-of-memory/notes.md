### Author Background
- **Frances A. Yates** (1899–1981): Distinguished British historian, Fellow of the Warburg Institute
- Known for groundbreaking work on Renaissance esotericism, Hermeticism, and Giordano Bruno
- Major works: *Giordano Bruno and the Hermetic Tradition* (1964), *The Art of Memory* (1966), *The Rosicrucian Enlightenment* (1972)
- Influenced by Aby Warburg's approach to cultural history and the afterlife of classical images
- *The Art of Memory* was chosen as one of Modern Library's 100 Best Nonfiction Books of the 20th Century
