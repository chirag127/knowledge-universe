**Author Background**: Nir Eyal is a former Stanford lecturer in behavioral design and author of the international bestseller *Hooked: How to Build Habit-Forming Products*. He writes about the intersection of psychology, technology, and business at NirAndFar.com. Julie Li contributed as a researcher and editor.

**Cover Design**: The cover features a line-drawn figure standing on top of a cliff or precipice, suggesting elevation above the noise — the "indistractable" state.
