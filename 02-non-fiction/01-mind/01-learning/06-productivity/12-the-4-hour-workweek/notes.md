**Author Background**: Timothy Ferriss is an American entrepreneur, investor, author, and podcaster. He graduated from Princeton University and built a supplement company (BrainQUICKEN) that funded his lifestyle design experiments. He is also known for *The 4-Hour Body*, *The 4-Hour Chef*, and *Tools of Titans*.

**Cover Design**: The cover features Ferris's face partially in a yellow caution sign — suggesting both warning and urgency.
