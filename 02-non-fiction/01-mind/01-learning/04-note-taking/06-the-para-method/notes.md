## 📝 Personal Notes

### Strengths
- Genuinely simple — can be explained in one sentence
- 60-second setup is not an exaggeration
- Platform agnostic — works in any tool
- The Projects vs. Areas distinction is genuinely clarifying
- Lightweight maintenance (weekly/monthly rhythm)
- Beautiful physical book design (noted by many reviewers)

### Weaknesses / Criticisms
- **Content overlap with BASB**: ~95% of PARA content already in *Building a Second Brain*
- **Too short**: Many reviews call it a "glorified blog post" or "booklet"
- **Low signal-to-noise**: ~74 pages of actual content padded to 208 with illustrations and whitespace
- **No new ideas for BASB readers**: Everything here was already published
- **Felt like a cash grab**: Numerous reviews mention this explicitly
- **No research or evidence**: Entirely opinion/experience-based

### Key Quotes
- "Organize as little as possible, as late as possible, and only as much as absolutely needed."
- "The key principle of PARA is simple: organize information by your projects and goals, not by the information's topic."
- "PARA is not just a way of organizing files. It's a way of organizing your life."

### Critical Review Consensus
- **Most positive reviews**: From beginners who haven't read *Building a Second Brain*
- **Most negative reviews**: From BASB readers who expected new material
- **Fair assessment**: Excellent booklet; overpriced as a hardcover book
- **Recommendation**: Read the free blog posts instead, or buy BASB which contains PARA plus the CODE method

### See Also
- **Author's Other Works**: *Building a Second Brain* (2022), *Life in Perspective* (upcoming)
