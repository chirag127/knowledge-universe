# Reading & Comprehension

Welcome to the **Reading & Comprehension** section of your Library of Alexandria. This branch covers speed reading techniques, deep comprehension systems, analytical reading, critical evaluation, and methods to maximize retention from textual materials.

---

## 🧠 Core Principles of Reading & Comprehension

1. **Inspectional vs. Analytical Reading**: Moving from a quick scan to get the layout (inspectional) to a deep, active breakdown of arguments and structures (analytical).
2. **Concept/Idea Extraction**: Breaking the habit of subvocalizing individual words and training the brain to process thoughts and visual phrases instead.
3. **Active Syntopical Reading**: Reading multiple texts on the same subject concurrently to extract, compare, and synthesize diverse perspectives.
4. **Metacognitive Questioning**: Constantly questioning assumptions, identifying biases, and evaluating structural arguments while reading.
