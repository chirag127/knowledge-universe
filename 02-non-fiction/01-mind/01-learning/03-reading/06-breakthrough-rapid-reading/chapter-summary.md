# Breakthrough Rapid Reading — Chapter-by-Chapter Summary

## Reading Guidance

This is not a book to read — it is a program to do. Complete one lesson per day
(~20 minutes). Do NOT skip exercises. Each lesson builds on the previous. Track
your progress with the rate-building drills. The 36 lessons are divided into
6 groups of 6, each group targeting a specific skill area.

---
