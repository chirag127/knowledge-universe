# Speed Reading with the Right Brain — Chapter-by-Chapter Summary

## Reading Guidance

This book is designed for sequential reading with practice. Each chapter builds on
the previous. Do the exercises — they are the core of the method. Read the
instructional chapters first, then practice with the formatted passages.
Re-read exercises multiple times for maximum benefit.
