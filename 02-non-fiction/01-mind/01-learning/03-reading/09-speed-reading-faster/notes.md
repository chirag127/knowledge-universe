### About the Authors

**Jan Cisek** was the first licensed PhotoReading (Speed Reading) instructor in the UK (2000). He has over 20 years of experience teaching speed reading worldwide, presenting at conferences, and running corporate training for organizations including BPP, Dorset Police, SEAL, IAL, Leeds University, and HSBC. He is a psychologist by training.

**Susan Norman** is a speed reading coach and world expert in accelerated learning. She has authored more than 50 books in the fields of English language teaching, NLP, and accelerated learning. Together, Cisek and Norman have run speed reading courses since 2004.

Their previous collaborative book: *Spd Rdng: The Speed Reading Bible*.
