# The Speed Reading Book — Chapter-by-Chapter Summary

## Reading Guidance

This book is designed as a self-paced course. Buzan recommends reading one
chapter per day and completing all exercises. The seven self-tests track
progress. Do not skip exercises — they build on each other. Use the progress
charts at the end of the book.
