### About the Authors

**Brian Tracy** is one of the world's leading authorities on personal development and human potential. He has written more than 80 books and produced hundreds of audio/video learning programs. His work focuses on leadership, sales, business success, and psychology.

**Colin Rose** is an internationally renowned expert in accelerated learning and the author of *Accelerate Your Learning* and the *Accelerated Learning Action Guide*. He draws on Nobel Prize-winning research (Roger Sperry's split-brain studies) to develop practical learning systems.

## 📝 Personal Notes

### Key Strengths of This Program
1. **Comprehensive scope** — covers learning, memory, reading, writing, and communication in one system
2. **Audio format** — ideal for commuters; the medium matches the message (multi-sensory learning)
3. **Nobel Prize research foundation** — references Sperry's split-brain work, Gardner's multiple intelligences
4. **The 6-stage framework** (Prepare → Acquire → Explore → Memorize → Demonstrate → Reflect) is universally applicable
5. **The Japanese counting exercise** brilliantly demonstrates accelerated learning in 3 minutes
6. **Baroque music integration** is a unique and well-researched addition

### Key Weaknesses / Criticisms
1. **Dated** — the program is from 1995; some brain science has moved on
2. **Audio-only format limitations** — harder to reference specific techniques compared to a book
3. **Speed reading coverage is brief** — only part of Session 9; buy a dedicated speed reading book for depth
4. **Self-promotion** — Tracy's characteristic motivational style can feel sales-oriented
5. **Scientifically controversial** — some claims (10% brain usage, Baroque music effects) are disputed
6. **High price** — $199-$240 for a 1995 program is expensive compared to modern alternatives
7. **New Age elements** — relaxation and visualization may not appeal to all readers

### Who Should Use This
- **Lifelong learners** wanting a complete learning system
- **Students** preparing for exams
- **Professionals** who need to continuously upskill
- **Parents and educators** wanting to teach children better learning methods

### Who Should Skip This
- **Pure speed reading seekers** — the reading content is only one session
- **Evidence-only readers** — the program mixes science with motivational speaking
- **Budget-conscious** — the $199+ price tag is steep

### Most Impactful Techniques (Top 3)
1. **6-Stage Learning Process** — a universal framework applicable to any subject
2. **Multi-Sensory Learning (VAK)** — understanding your learning style transforms how you study
3. **17 Memory Methods** — a comprehensive toolkit for any memorization task
