### About the Authors

- **Barbara Oakley, PhD**: Professor of Engineering at Oakland
University; Ramón y Cajal Distinguished Scholar of Global Digital
Learning at McMaster University. Created the world's most popular MOOC
"Learning How to Learn." Holds a PhD in Systems Engineering. Previously
served in the US Army as a language officer.
- **Terrence Sejnowski, PhD**: Francis Crick Professor at the Salk
Institute for Biological Studies; Director of the Computational
Neurobiology Laboratory. Elected to the National Academy of Sciences,
National Academy of Medicine, and National Academy of Engineering.
Pioneer in neural networks and computational neuroscience.
- **Alistair McConville**: Director of Learning and Innovation at
Bedales School (UK). Educator who helped adapt the adult content of
*A Mind for Numbers* for a younger audience.
