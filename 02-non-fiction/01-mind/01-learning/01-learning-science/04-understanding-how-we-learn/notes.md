### About the Authors

- **Yana Weinstein (Jude Weinstein-Jones)**: Former Assistant Professor of
Psychology at University of Massachusetts Lowell. Co-founder of The
Learning Scientists. Now known as Jude Weinstein-Jones. PhD in
cognitive psychology.
- **Megan Sumeracki (formerly Smith)**: Former Assistant Professor of
Psychology at Rhode Island College. Co-founder of The Learning
Scientists. PhD in cognitive psychology. Focuses on translating
cognitive science research for educational practitioners.
- **Oliver Caviglioli**: Educational illustrator and former headteacher.
Specialises in visual learning, dual coding, and sketchnoting.
Designed all illustrations in the book.
