# Book Summary: The Cambridge Handbook of Expertise and Expert Performance

## Overview
This massive reference work compiles the theoretical consensus on expert performance. It surveys historical theories of skill acquisition, psychometric approaches, brain imaging research, superior working memory capacities in experts (Long-Term Working Memory theory), and the structural limits of experience. It acts as the definitive academic bible on how deliberate practice re-structures human physiology and cognition.

---

### Core Learning Takeaways
* **Pillar 1**: The comprehensive academic reference textbook consolidating global scientific research on how expertise is defined, measured, acquired, and maintained across cognitive and motor domains.
* **Pillar 2**: Real-world application of memory, consolidation, and practice.
* **Pillar 3**: Overcoming study illusions through active, effortful retrieval.
