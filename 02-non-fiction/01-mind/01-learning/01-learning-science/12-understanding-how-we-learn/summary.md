# Book Summary: Understanding How We Learn: A Visual Guide

## Overview
Translates empirical cognitive psychology into six fundamental learning practices: Spaced Practice, Interleaving, Elaboration, Concrete Examples, Dual Coding, and Retrieval Practice. The authors bridge the gap between educational theory and real-world implementation, arguing that subjective study intuition is often a poor guide for durable retention.

---

### Core Learning Takeaways
* **Pillar 1**: Explores the psychological theories behind six fundamental strategies for effective learning, helping students and educators navigate evidence-based learning science.
* **Pillar 2**: Real-world application of memory, consolidation, and practice.
* **Pillar 3**: Overcoming study illusions through active, effortful retrieval.
