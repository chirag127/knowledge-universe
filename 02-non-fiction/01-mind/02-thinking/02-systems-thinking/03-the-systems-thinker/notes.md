## Author Background

Albert Rutherford is a pseudonymous author who has written
extensively in the "thinking skills" space. Other works
include *The Art of Thinking Critically*, *The Analytical
Thinker*, and *The Systems Thinker Series* (multiple
volumes). The author appears to focus on summarizing and
repackaging established intellectual frameworks for a mass-
market audience.

## Personal Notes

- This is a derivative work — no original research or novel
framework.
- Best used as an entry-level companion to Senge and
Meadows, not a replacement.
- The exercise sections are genuinely useful for
building systems thinking habits.
- Chapter-specific references would be a significant
improvement for the serious reader.
- The book does not cite specific page numbers for the
concepts it draws from — a notable scholarly weakness.
