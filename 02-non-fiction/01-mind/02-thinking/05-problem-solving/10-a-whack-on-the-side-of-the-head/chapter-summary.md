# A Whack on the Side of the Head — Chapter-by-Chapter Summary

## Reading Guidance

The book is structured around 10 "mental locks" — each chapter addresses one lock. Read them in order or jump to the lock that resonates most. Each chapter follows the same pattern: the lock described, why it blocks creativity, stories/puzzles that "whack" you out of it, and the key to unlock it.
