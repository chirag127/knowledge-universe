## 📝 Personal Notes

### Why This Book Matters
The most accessible and comprehensive single-volume catalog of mental models. Perfect for someone who wants to learn about many models quickly without deep mathematical or philosophical immersion.

### Key Strengths
- Exceptional breadth (200+ models in one book)
- Beautiful illustrations make it memorable
- Thematic organization is intuitive and practical
- Author credibility (Weinberg founded a successful company)

### Key Limitations
- Minimal depth per model (1-2 pages average)
- Some models are oversimplified
- Less rigorous than Page, Parrish, or Bevelin
- Some entries feel like dictionary definitions rather than insights

### Best Read With
- *The Great Mental Models* series for deeper treatment of core models
- *The Model Thinker* for mathematical rigor
- *Poor Charlie's Almanack* for original source material

### Reading Time
- Total: ~5-8 hours
- Per chapter: ~30-60 minutes
