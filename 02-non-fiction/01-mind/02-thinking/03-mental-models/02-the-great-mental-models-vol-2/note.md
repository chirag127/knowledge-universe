## 📝 Personal Notes

### Why This Book Matters
Translates hard science into practical wisdom. Fills a critical gap in the mental models library that Vol. 1 didn't cover. Essential for understanding the physical/biological forces that shape outcomes.

### Key Strengths
- Makes complex scientific concepts accessible without dumbing them down
- Bridges the gap between scientific literacy and practical decision-making
- Each model is grounded in real, verifiable science

### Key Limitations
- Some models are more metaphorically than scientifically applied
- Readers with strong science backgrounds may find coverage shallow
- Less immediately practical than Vol. 1's general thinking concepts

### Best Read With
- Volume 1 (General Thinking Concepts) for foundation
- Volume 3 (Systems and Mathematics) for the next level
- *Poor Charlie's Almanack* for Munger's original thinking on science models

### Reading Time
- Total: ~5-7 hours
- Per chapter: ~20-30 minutes
